How you can help

Help us in making a difference! Here are some ways you can contribute to preserving our planet:

  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle.
  • Volunteer for cleanups in your community.
  • Conserve power and electricity.
  • Plant a tree.
  • Educate others about enviromental conservation.
